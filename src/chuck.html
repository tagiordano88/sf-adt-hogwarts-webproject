Below are some pictures from Disney Land. I went for the first time in 2019. I didn't know what to expect, but I was pleasantly surprised. We went for three days and had a park hopper pass. The first day we went and experienced many of the rides. Some of the rides that I really enjoyed were Radiator Springs Racers, Matterhorn Bobsleds, and Splash Mountain. Soaring around the world was pretty amazing to. I was impressed with the cleanliness of the park and the food was pretty good to. I had several corn dogs that were recommended by friend that had gone in the past and I wasn't disappointed. The rides were put together so well and I was hooked after my first time going. I'm usually skeptical when it comes to spending money at theme parks, but Disney Land was worth every penny.
